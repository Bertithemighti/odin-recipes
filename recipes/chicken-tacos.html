Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds of chicken breast
  • Organic corn tortilla's from Trader Joes
  • Shredded iceberg lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onions
  • Jalepenos
  • Cilantro
  • Limes,or lime juice

Steps

  1. Season with spices of your choice, and cook your chicken breast
  2. dice up all of your veggies
  3. mix your diced veggies with your lime juice with basic salt and pepper
  4. Microwave your tortillas
  5. lay your preffered amount of chicken and your newfound pico de gallo making skills and enjoy your chicken tacos!
