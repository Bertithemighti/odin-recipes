Berti Chilli

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • crushed red tomatoes
  • chicken broth
  • garbanzo beans(i just like em (radda)
  • red bell peppers(diced)
  • McCormick chilli powder
  • Onions

Steps

  1. cook about 10 ounces of ground turkey
  2. add onion to your ground turkey as it cooks(if ya like em)
  3. use a slow cooker
  4. let it all slow cook for four hours
