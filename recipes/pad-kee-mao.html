Pad-Kee-Mao

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Diced red Chilli pepper
  • minced garlic
  • Green onions
  • Onions
  • Fish Sauce
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Rice noodles
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Mix together your sauces along with your sugar
  2. add oil to your pan, and start to simmer your garlic and diced chilli
  3. prepare your preferred rice noodles
  4. combine all sauces, cook your chicken as well
  5. I prefrer cooking it ah a high temperture, but if you want it to be more steamy, use water when cooking with your veggies and noodles
